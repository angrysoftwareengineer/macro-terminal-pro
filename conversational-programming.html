CNC Conversational programming

A lot of people think conversational programming is just for basic, routine tasks. But with CNC Macro Simulator II, it’s a game-changer packed with benefits. Here’s why it really stands out:

1. All-in-One Exporting

When you export a conversational program, it saves everything you need in one file—tools, their specs, selected processes, workpiece data, and even machine types.

2. Custom Operations

While CNC Macro Simulator II has plenty of pre-built operations, you can easily create your own using Fanuc Macro B or Centroid standards. Mix and match as you like; the only limit is your imagination!

3. Flexible Workflow Management

With CNC Macro Simulator II, you can rearrange operations, copy, remove, or activate/deactivate them all with a click. Work smarter, not harder!

4. Quick Cycle Builder

The built-in cycle builder lets you whip up custom operations or macro programs in no time. Want to test a subroutine? The builder generates a main program that includes a call to it, complete with the right variables and linked subroutines.

5. Adjustable Default Settings

You can customize the default settings to fit your specific needs.

6. Custom Code Integration

Need to tweak an operation or add your own code? The "Code Snippet" operation makes it easy to integrate your custom code.

7. Easy Operation Search

Finding operations is a breeze with the handy search bar.

8. Versatile Export Options

Export your conversational program in formats like ISO, Heidenhain, Sinumerik, or Okuma.
